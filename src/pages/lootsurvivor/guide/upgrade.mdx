# Upgrade Guide

Leveling up and upgrading your adventurer is a pivotal moment in every Loot Survivor run. Each level brings stat points to allocate and opens the market with fresh items. Master the upgrade system to transform your adventurer from a fragile newcomer into an unstoppable force!

> **⚡ Power Spike:** Every level up is an opportunity to dramatically increase your power. Plan your upgrades carefully!

## Upgrade Overview

The upgrade system consists of three key components:

1. **Stat Allocation** - Distribute points to enhance your adventurer's capabilities
2. **Market Refresh** - Access new items with each level up
3. **Purchase Decisions** - Buy equipment and potions from the market

# Stat Allocation

## Stat Point Distribution

You receive **1 stat point per level** to allocate among:

| Stat     | Effect per Point               | Primary Use         |
| -------- | ------------------------------ | ------------------- |
| **STR**  | +10% attack damage             | Combat damage       |
| **DEX**  | Improves flee success          | Escaping from beasts |
| **VIT**  | +15 HP max & current           | Health and survival |
| **INT**  | Aids obstacle avoidance        | Avoiding obstacles  |
| **WIS**  | Helps evade beast ambushes     | Ambush prevention   |
| **CHA**  | Gold discount on items/potions | Market purchases    |
| **LUCK** | ❌ Cannot upgrade              | Item discovery only |

> **🎯 Allocation Note:** Consider focusing on 2-3 primary stats rather than spreading points evenly across all stats.

## Multi-Levelling System

### What is Multi-Levelling?

In Loot Survivor, it's possible to **level up multiple times in a single explore action**. This powerful mechanic can dramatically accelerate your adventurer's progression when you gain large amounts of experience at once.

### Stat Point Allocation

**🔢 Multiple Level Formula:**
- **Stat Points = Number of Levels Gained**
- Level up 3 times → Get 3 stat points to allocate
- Level up 5 times → Get 5 stat points to allocate

> **💡 Strategic Tip:** Multi-levelling allows you to make significant stat investments in a single upgrade session, enabling focused builds and powerful specialization.

### Market Size Expansion

The market dramatically expands with multi-levelling:

| Levels Gained | Market Size | Items Available |
|---------------|-------------|-----------------|
| **1 Level**   | 20 items    | Standard selection |
| **2+ Levels** | Up to 101 items | Massive selection |

**🛍️ Market Benefits:**
- **Expanded Inventory:** More weapons, armor, and potions
- **Better Odds:** Higher chance of finding specific item types
- **Tier Variety:** Access to items across multiple tiers
- **Strategic Choices:** More options for optimizing your build

> **⚡ Power Spike Alert:** Multi-levelling creates the most significant power increases in the game. The combination of multiple stat points plus an expanded market can transform a struggling adventurer into a dominant force!
