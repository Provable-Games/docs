## Distribution Breakdown

### Community Allocation: 7,500,000 (75%)

The majority of SURVIVOR tokens are allocated to the community through various mechanisms designed to reward participation, contribution, and loyalty to the ecosystem.

#### DCA & Treasury: 2,500,000 (25%)
- **DCA Distribution**: 2,000,000 (20%) - Tokens distributed via Dollar Cost Averaging orders to ensure fair market distribution and price discovery. Proceeds from the DCA are allocated to the governance treasury
- **Treasury Reserve**: 500,000 (5%) - SURVIVOR tokens reserved for future ecosystem development, strategic partnerships, and unforeseen opportunities. These tokens along with DCA proceeds are controlled by SURVIVOR token holders

#### Dungeon Rewards: 2,500,000 (25%)
- **LS2 Dungeon**: 2,000,000 (20%) - Rewards for players participating in the main Loot Survivor 2 game mode
- **Alt-Dungeons**: 500,000 (5%) - Incentives for alternative game modes, special events, and experimental features

#### Ecosystem Partners: 1,047,070 (10.47%)
Strategic allocations to key contributors and ecosystem partners:
- **Biblio**: 500,000 (5%) - Knowledge base, lore, and documentation contributions
- **Budokan**: 261,035 (2.61%) - Combat system development and balancing
- **Summit**: 261,035 (2.61%) - Achievement systems and progression mechanics
- **Casey**: 25,000 (0.25%) - Community initiatives and engagement

#### Beast Ecosystem: 1,002,930 (10.03%)
Supporting the Beast NFT ecosystem within Loot Survivor:
- **Beast Mints**: 931,500 (9.3%) - Incentives for new Beast NFT minting
- **LS1 2381 Beasts**: 71,430 (0.71%) - Rewards for the 2,381 legacy Beast holders from Loot Survivor v1

#### Collection Rewards: 450,000 (4.5%)
Recognition for early supporters and collectors:
- **Loot**: 250,000 (2.5%) - Original Loot NFT holders
- **GA (Genesis Adventurers)**: 100,000 (1%) - Genesis Adventurer holders
- **Skulls**: 100,000 (1%) - Skull NFT holders

### Team Allocation: 2,500,000 (25%)

Reserved for the Loot Survivor development team to ensure continued protocol development, maintenance, and growth. This allocation aligns long-term incentives between the team and the ecosystem's success.

## Value Accrual Mechanisms

### Governance Treasury
SURVIVOR token holders control the governance treasury, which consists of:
- Proceeds from the DCA token sale (ETH, STRK, USDC)
- 80% of proceeds from dungeon ticket purchases
- 500,000 SURVIVOR tokens from the treasury reserve

These funds can be directed towards:
- Buyback and burns to distribute game revenue to token holders
- Development funding for new features
- Liquidity provision incentives
- Ecosystem partnerships and growth initiatives


### Governance Rights
Token holders control the governance treasury through:
- Treasury allocation decisions
- Partnership approvals and funding
- Community initiative funding
- Strategic use of DCA proceeds
- Management of the 500,000 treasury reserve tokens

## Distribution Schedule

### Immediate Distribution
- Airdrop allocations: Claimable immediately upon launch
- Collection rewards: Available for eligible holders to claim

### Phased Distribution
- DCA orders: Executed over predetermined periods for fair price discovery
- Dungeon rewards: Distributed based on gameplay achievements and milestones
- Beast mints: Released according to minting events and schedules

### Vesting Considerations
Different allocations may have specific vesting or unlock schedules:
- Community rewards may be immediate or activity-based
- Team allocation subject to long-term alignment agreements
- Treasury reserve tokens and DCA proceeds released through token holder governance proposals

## Economic Sustainability

The tokenomics model is designed to create a self-sustaining ecosystem where:
- Players are rewarded for skill and participation
- Developers are incentivized to build on the platform
- Token holders benefit from ecosystem growth
- Protocol revenue supports ongoing development

This balanced approach ensures long-term viability while maintaining decentralization and community ownership.
