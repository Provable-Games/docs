# Stats

Understanding and optimizing your adventurer's stats is crucial for survival in Loot Survivor. Each of the seven core stats plays a vital role in different aspects of gameplay, from combat effectiveness to market efficiency. Master stat allocation to create powerful builds tailored to your playstyle!

## Stats Overview

<div style={{ background: '#1a2332', padding: '1.5rem', borderRadius: '8px', border: '2px solid #3b82f6', margin: '2rem 0', lineHeight: '1.8' }}>

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1rem' }}>

Every adventurer has **7 core stats** that define their capabilities:

</div>

<div style={{ marginBottom: '1rem' }}>

- **Physical Stats**: Strength, Dexterity, Vitality
- **Mental Stats**: Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma
- **Metaphysical**: Luck (special stat with unique mechanics)

</div>

> **📈 Progression Note:** You gain 1 stat point per level to allocate freely, except for Luck which can only be increased through items!

</div>

## The Seven Core Stats

### Physical Stats

<div style={{ display: 'grid', gridTemplateColumns: 'repeat(auto-fit, minmax(300px, 1fr))', gap: '1rem', margin: '2rem 0' }}>

<div style={{ background: '#2a1a1a', padding: '1.5rem', borderRadius: '8px', border: '2px solid #dc2626' }}>

#### 💪 Strength (STR)

**Primary Effect:** Boosts attack damage by 10%

| Aspect            | Details                   |
| ----------------- | ------------------------- |
| **Damage Bonus**  | +10% attack damage        |
| **Weapon Types**  | All physical weapons      |
| **Scaling**       | Linear (2-3 damage/point) |
| **Max Effective** | No cap                    |

**Build Synergy:**

- Pairs with VIT for tank builds
- Combines with LUCK for crit builds
- Essential for melee fighters

> **⚔️ Pro Tip:** 10 STR doubles your attack damage output!

</div>

<div style={{ background: '#1a2a2a', padding: '1.5rem', borderRadius: '8px', border: '2px solid #10b981' }}>

#### 🏃 Dexterity (DEX)

**Primary Effect:** Improves chances of successfully fleeing

| Aspect           | Details                |
| ---------------- | ---------------------- |
| **Flee Success** | DEX/Level ratio        |
| **Base Formula** | Success% = DEX/Level   |
| **Scaling**      | Linear with ratio      |
| **Future**       | Will affect initiative |

**Flee Success Formula:**

```
Success% = (DEX / Level) × 100
```

> **🏃 Escape Artist:** DEX equal to your level = guaranteed escape!

</div>

<div style={{ background: '#1a1a3e', padding: '1.5rem', borderRadius: '8px', border: '2px solid #a855f7' }}>

#### ❤️ Vitality (VIT)

**Primary Effect:** Increases max health and current by +15HP

| Aspect       | Details                  |
| ------------ | ------------------------ |
| **HP Bonus** | +15 HP per point         |
| **Base HP**  | 100 at level 1           |
| **Healing**  | Also heals when upgraded |
| **Scaling**  | Unlimited                |

**Health Calculation:**

```
Max HP = 100 + (VIT × 15)
```

| VIT | Total HP | Survivability |
| --- | -------- | ------------- |
| 0   | 100      | Fragile       |
| 10  | 250      | Sturdy        |
| 20  | 400      | Tanky         |
| 30  | 550      | Unkillable    |

> **🛡️ Tank Secret:** VIT upgrades heal you instantly - use strategically!

</div>

</div>

### Mental Stats

<div style={{ display: 'grid', gridTemplateColumns: 'repeat(auto-fit, minmax(300px, 1fr))', gap: '1rem', margin: '2rem 0' }}>

<div style={{ background: '#2a1a3e', padding: '1.5rem', borderRadius: '8px', border: '2px solid #a855f7' }}>

#### 🧠 Intelligence (INT)

**Primary Effect:** Aids in avoiding obstacles

| Aspect                 | Details                             |
| ---------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| **Obstacle Avoidance** | INT/Level ratio                     |
| **Base Formula**       | Success% = INT/Level                |
| **Application**        | Environmental hazards               |
| **Special**            | Complete obstacle immunity possible |

**Obstacle Avoidance Formula:**

```
Avoidance% = (INT / Level) × 100
```

| INT/Level Ratio | Avoidance Rate |
| --------------- | -------------- |
| 0.5             | 50%            |
| 1.0             | 100%           |
| 1.5             | 100% (capped)  |
| 2.0+            | 100% (capped)  |

> **🧠 Intelligence Power:** High INT builds can completely avoid obstacle damage!

</div>

<div style={{ background: '#2a2a1a', padding: '1.5rem', borderRadius: '8px', border: '2px solid #eab308' }}>

#### 📚 Wisdom (WIS)

**Primary Effect:** Helps evade beast ambushes and improves exploration

| Aspect             | Details                  |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ |
| **Ambush Evasion** | Level-based formula      |
| **Exploration**    | Better discovery rates   |
| **Future**         | Spell cooldowns, secrets |
| **Scaling**        | Percentage-based         |

**Ambush Avoidance Formula:**

```
Evasion% = (WIS / Level) × 100
```

| WIS | At Level 10 | At Level 20 |
| --- | ----------- | ----------- |
| 5   | 50%         | 25%         |
| 10  | 100%        | 50%         |
| 15  | 150% (cap)  | 75%         |

> **👁️ Foresight:** High WIS reveals hidden treasures and paths!

</div>

<div style={{ background: '#3e2a1a', padding: '1.5rem', borderRadius: '8px', border: '2px solid #f59e0b' }}>

#### 😎 Charisma (CHA)

**Primary Effect:** Gives gold discount on items and health potions

| Aspect           | Details          |
| ---------------- | ---------------- |
| **Discount**     | -1% per point    |
| **Max Discount** | ~50% practical   |
| **Applies To**   | All market items |
| **Future**       | NPC interactions |

**Price Calculation:**

```
Final Price = Base Price × (1 - CHA/100)
```

| CHA | Discount | 100g Item | 50g Potion |
| --- | -------- | --------- | ---------- |
| 0   | 0%       | 100g      | 50g        |
| 10  | 10%      | 90g       | 45g        |
| 20  | 20%      | 80g       | 40g        |
| 30  | 30%      | 70g       | 35g        |

> **💰 Economy Master:** 30 CHA saves 30% on everything!

</div>

</div>

### Metaphysical Stat

<div style={{ background: '#2a1a2a', padding: '2rem', borderRadius: '8px', border: '2px solid #10b981', margin: '2rem 0' }}>

#### 🍀 Luck (LUCK)

**The Special Stat** - Raises chances of critical damage and cannot be upgraded directly!

| Aspect              | Details           |
| ------------------- | ----------------- |
| **Critical Chance** | LUCK / 100        |
| **Base Crit**       | 0% at 0 LUCK      |
| **Max Crit**        | 100% at 100 LUCK  |
| **Loot Quality**    | Better drop rates |
| **How to Increase** | Equipment only    |

**Critical Hit Mechanics:**

```
Crit Chance = LUCK / 100
Crit Damage = Base Damage × 2
Max LUCK = 100 (100% crit chance)
```

<div style={{ display: 'grid', gridTemplateColumns: 'repeat(2, 1fr)', gap: '1rem', margin: '1rem 0' }}>

<div style={{ background: '#1a1a1a', padding: '1rem', borderRadius: '8px' }}>

**Luck Sources:**

- Jewelry items (Rings, Amulets)
- "of Fortune" suffix items
- Special legendary items
- Temporary buffs (future)

</div>

<div style={{ background: '#1a1a1a', padding: '1rem', borderRadius: '8px' }}>

**Crit Chance Examples:**
| LUCK | Crit Chance | Combat Impact |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| 0    | 0%          | No crits      |
| 25   | 25%         | 1 in 4 hits   |
| 50   | 50%         | Half your hits|
| 75   | 75%         | 3 in 4 hits   |
| 100  | 100%        | Every hit!    |

</div>

</div>

> **🎰 RNG Master:** Stack LUCK items for massive critical damage and legendary drops!

</div>

## Conclusion

Stats are the foundation of every successful adventurer in Loot Survivor. Whether you choose to be an unstoppable tank, a glass cannon dealing massive damage, or a nimble escape artist, understanding stat mechanics is key to survival. Remember: there's no single "best" build - the optimal stat distribution depends on your playstyle, the items you find, and your ability to adapt to the dungeon's challenges.

> **🏆 Final Wisdom:** The best stat allocation is one that keeps you alive and progressing. When in doubt, add VIT!
