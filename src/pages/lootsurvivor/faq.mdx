# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

<div style={{ background: '#1a1a1a', padding: '1.5rem', borderRadius: '8px', margin: '1rem 0' }}>

### 🎮 What is Loot Survivor?

Loot Survivor is a fully onchain dungeon crawler RPG built on Starknet. Every action, from combat to loot generation, happens directly on the blockchain, making the game transparent, verifiable, and permanent.

> **Key Point:** Your achievements live forever on the blockchain!

</div>

### Is Loot Survivor free to play?

Yes Loot Survivor has a completely free game mode for players to practice their skills. For the full dungeon experience a ticket is required to enter which is purchased from an Ekubo dca pool. No gas required.

Yes! Loot Survivor has a fully responsive design with a dedicated mobile interface optimized for touch controls and smaller screens.

## Gameplay Questions

### How do I start playing?

Just choose your game mode and click "New Game" to start exploring! See the [Getting Started Guide](/lootsurvivor/guide/getting-started) for detailed instructions.

### What happens when my adventurer dies?

Death is permanent - your adventurer's journey ends but earned assets remain onchain forever. Start a new adventure anytime. Learn more in the [Getting Started Guide](/lootsurvivor/guide/getting-started).

### Can I have multiple adventurers?

Yes! Each adventurer is a separate NFT with unique progression. See [Getting Started Guide](/lootsurvivor/guide/getting-started) for details.

### ⚔️ How does combat work?

Turn-based combat with rock-paper-scissors type advantages (±50% damage). Each weapon/armor type beats one and loses to another.

See detailed guides: [Battle Guide](/lootsurvivor/guide/battle) • [Combat Mechanics](/lootsurvivor/combat)

### 📊 What are the different stats and what do they do?

Seven core stats: STR (damage), DEX (flee), VIT (HP), INT (obstacles), WIS (ambush), CHA (prices), LUCK (crits). Each affects combat and exploration differently.

Full details: [Stats Guide](/lootsurvivor/stats)

### How do items work?

5 tiers (T5 common → T1 legendary). Items level up and unlock suffixes (Lv15) and prefixes (Lv19+) for bonuses.

See: [Loot Guide](/lootsurvivor/loot)

### What's the best strategy?

No single best strategy! Focus on type advantages, stat balance, tactical decisions, and resource management.

Strategies covered in: [Battle Guide](/lootsurvivor/guide/battle) • [Getting Started](/lootsurvivor/guide/getting-started)

## Technical Questions

<div style={{ background: '#2a1a3e', padding: '1.5rem', borderRadius: '8px', border: '1px solid #a855f7', margin: '1rem 0' }}>

### ⛓️ What blockchain is Loot Survivor on?

Loot Survivor runs on **Starknet**, a Layer 2 scaling solution for Ethereum.

| Feature         | Benefit                  |
| --------------- | ------------------------ |
| **Low Fees**    | < $0.01 per transaction  |
| **High Speed**  | 1-3 second confirmations |
| **Security**    | Ethereum-level security  |
| **Scalability** | Thousands of TPS         |

</div>

### Are the game outcomes truly random?

Uses VRF (Verifiable Random Function) seeds by default for true randomness. Can be configured to use deterministic seeds in settings.

Technical details: [Architecture Overview](/lootsurvivor/technical/architecture)

### Can I verify the game logic?

Yes! All contracts are open source and verified on Starknet. You can inspect the code on [GitHub](https://github.com/Provable-Games/death-mountain) and verify any game action onchain.

### What is Dojo?

Starknet game engine framework using Entity-Component-System architecture for efficient onchain state management.

Learn more: [Technical Architecture](/lootsurvivor/technical/architecture)

### How are gas fees kept low?

Optimizations: packed storage, batch operations, efficient algorithms, plus Starknet's L2 scaling benefits.

Details: [Technical Architecture](/lootsurvivor/technical/architecture)

## Economic Questions

### What are LORDS tokens?

LORDS is the native token of the Realms ecosystem. In Loot Survivor, LORDS can be used for:

- Entry fees for special game modes
- Tournament entries

### How does the market work?

The in-game market:

- Refreshes when you level up
- Uses VRF seeds for item generation
- Prices decrease with Charisma
- No real money transactions between players

### Can I trade items with other players?

Currently, direct player-to-player trading is not implemented. All trading happens through the in-game market system.

## Wallet & Account Questions

### Which wallets are supported?

**For VRF (Tournament) Mode:**
- Cartridge Controller (required for VRF compatibility)

**For Standard Mode:**
- Any Starknet-compatible wallet (Argent X, Braavos, etc.)

### I lost access to my wallet. Can I recover my adventurer?

If you lose wallet access, you lose access to your adventurers. Always keep your recovery phrase secure and backed up.

### Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes! Your adventurers are tied to your wallet address, not your device. You can play from any device where you can access your wallet.

### Why do transactions sometimes fail?

Common reasons:

- Network congestion
- Wallet not properly connected

### Can I build on top of Loot Survivor?

Yes! Loot Survivor is open source and designed to be composable. You can:

- Build custom frontends
- Create analytical tools
- Extend game mechanics
- Integrate with other protocols

### How do I integrate Loot Survivor into my app?

See our [Integration Guide](/lootsurvivor/technical/integration) for detailed instructions on building with Loot Survivor's contracts.

### What's the maximum level?

There's no hard level cap. The theoretical maximum depends on your ability to survive increasingly difficult challenges.

### How are beasts generated?

Beasts are generated from VRF seeds based on:

- Your adventurer's level
- A random seed value
- Tier distribution tables

See: [Combat Mechanics](/lootsurvivor/combat)

### Can I predict market items?

No - market items use VRF seeds for true randomness (unless configured otherwise in settings).

See: [Technical Architecture](/lootsurvivor/technical/architecture)

## Legal & Safety

### Is Loot Survivor audited?

The smart contracts have not udergone an official audit yet. As with all blockchain applications, use at your own risk.

### Is this gambling?

Loot Survivor is a skill-based game with random elements. The outcome depends on player decisions and strategy, not purely chance.

### What data is collected?

Only onchain data (transactions, game actions) is recorded. No personal information is collected or stored off-chain.

### Can I lose money playing?

You pay entry fees for certain game modes. Never spend more than you can afford to lose.
